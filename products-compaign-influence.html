Campaign Influence

Track campaign performance in Salesforce—to get a complete picture of how marketing influences revenue

  • watch a demo ›
  • See How it Works ›
  • our products:
  • Response Management
  • Campaign Influence
  • Opportunity Gatekeeper
  • Easy Campaign Member Status

At Full Circle, we want to arm marketers with rock-solid campaign performance data and complete confidence in how their campaigns impact the bottom line. But to get there you need insight into how campaigns influence pipeline and revenue. Visibility into each campaign that creates and touches every Opportunity is critical to reveal just how much marketing contributes to revenue. That’s why we transformed how you track campaign performance in Salesforce - to get a complete end-to-end picture of how your marketing programs influence revenue, letting you weight your campaign touches by the different criteria that make sense for your organization.

Available separately or included in Full Circle Response Management.
